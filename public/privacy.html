Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Meeting Summarizer AI only uses your Google account to authenticate you and access your calendar events for summarization purposes. We do not share or sell your data.

  • We only access your calendar events with your permission.
  • Your meeting data is used solely for generating summaries and is not stored after processing.
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties.
  • You can revoke access at any time from your Google account settings.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at support@shekla.web.app.
